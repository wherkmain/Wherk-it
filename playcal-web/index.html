Frequently Asked Questions

How much does PlayCal cost?

PlayCal will be $5-10/month per family after launch. Join the waitlist now for 50% off your first year. We're still finalizing pricing based on parent feedback.

Is my child's information private?

Absolutely. Your child's availability is only visible to parents you explicitly invite. We don't have public profiles or discovery features. We're COPPA, GDPR, and CCPA compliant.

Do both parents need to pay?

No. One subscription covers your entire family, including co-parents and caregivers you invite. They get full access at no extra cost.

How does party invitation tracking work?

Simply forward your Evite or Paperless Post emails to your unique PlayCal address. Our AI extracts the event details and adds it to your calendar with RSVP reminders.

Is this a court-mandated co-parenting app?

No. PlayCal is designed for social scheduling, not legal documentation. If you need court-admissible records, apps like OurFamilyWizard are better suited.

When will PlayCal launch?

We're targeting Spring 2026. Waitlist members will get early access to beta test the app before public launch.
