# Scout - SEO Research & Domain Name Recommendations

## SEO Keyword Research Summary

### What People Search For

**Primary Keywords (High Intent):**
- "restock alerts" / "back in stock alerts"
- "price tracker" / "price monitoring"
- "availability monitor" / "product tracker"
- "sneaker monitor" / "sneaker bot"
- "card price tracker" / "sports card alerts"
- "find hard to get items" / "rare item finder"

**Secondary Keywords:**
- "ebay price tracker"
- "stockx monitor"
- "collectible alerts"
- "limited release tracker"
- "product availability checker"

### Competitive Landscape

**Existing Players:**
| Tool | Focus | Gap |
|------|-------|-----|
| Visualping | General product monitoring | No AI/natural language, no collectible focus |
| NowInStock.net | Tech/gaming restocks | No natural language search |
| HotStock | Consumer electronics | Limited to major retailers |
| TrackaLacker | General products | No AI matching, basic monitoring |
| Market Movers/CollX/Card Ladder | Sports card prices | PRICE tracking, not AVAILABILITY alerts |
| Sneaker bots (Sneakers AIO, etc.) | Sneaker drops | Complex setup, not for collectors |

**The Gap:** Nobody combines:
- AI-powered natural language search
- Multi-marketplace monitoring (eBay, Mercari, StockX, COMC)
- Instant alerts for collectibles specifically
- Easy setup (no URLs needed)

### SEO Opportunity

**Target long-tail keywords:**
- "find [player] cards under $[price]"
- "get alerted when [item] is listed"
- "monitor ebay for [specific card]"
- "sneaker restock alerts [brand]"

**Content strategy:**
- "How to find rare [cards/sneakers/items]"
- "Best way to monitor eBay for deals"
- "How to never miss a card listing"

## Top 5 Recommendations

### 1. **skowt.app** ⭐ (BEST)
- Modern .app TLD (Google registry, HTTPS-only)
- Alternative spelling that's still pronounceable
- Available (based on DNS check)
- Distinctive, brandable
- Appeals to tech-savvy collectors

### 2. **skout.io** ⭐
- Clean, short, memorable
- .io is popular with tech/dev tools
- "Skout" differentiates from generic "scout"
- Sounds like a startup

### 3. **restockscout.com**
- SEO gold: contains "restock" (high search volume)
- Clear what the product does
- Trustworthy .com
- May rank faster for target keywords

### 4. **rarefindr.com**
- "Findr" is startup-friendly spelling
- Clear value prop: finding rare items
- Broad appeal (not just cards/sneakers)
- Memorable

### 5. **itemhunt.io**
- "Hunt" implies active searching
- ProductHunt-style naming
- Broad enough to expand beyond collectibles
- .io gives tech credibility

---

## My Recommendation

**Go with skowt.app or skout.io**

**Why:**
1. Your target market (collectors, sneakerheads) skews younger and tech-savvy
2. Alternative spellings are common in startup culture (Flickr, Tumblr, Lyft)
3. "Skowt/Skout" sounds like a verb: "I'll skowt that for you"
4. Stands out from generic "scout" names
5. Modern TLDs (.app, .io) signal innovation

**Fallback:** If you want SEO traction faster, go with **restockscout.com** — you'll rank for "restock alerts" more easily.
